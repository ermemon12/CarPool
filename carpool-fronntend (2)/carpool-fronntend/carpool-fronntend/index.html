Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered about carpooling

What is carpooling?

+

Carpooling allows multiple people to share a single vehicle for their journey, reducing costs, saving fuel, and helping the environment.

How can I join a carpool?

+

To join, sign up on our platform, enter your location and destination, and find a ride that fits your schedule.

Is carpooling safe?

+

Yes, all drivers are verified, and our system includes ratings and reviews to help ensure a safe experience.

What are the costs?

+

The cost is split among passengers, making it cheaper than driving alone or using public transportation.

How does the carpooling app work?

+

Sign in, choose your role as a driver or passenger, enter your route, and find available carpools. It's that easy!
